One day, three friends got together and complained.

“Companies talk a lot about diversity, but they don’t walk the talk,” the first friend said. “How many more job/inudstry horror stories do we have to hear from other people of color before something changes?” the second friend sighed. The third friend quipped, “I wish there were a place that listed companies that are good for people of color to work at. I hate that all of our friends have had such terrible experiences.”

We are those three friends: Amélie Lamont, Catt Small and Jacky Alciné. Turning frustration into action, we bought some domains and created Good for PoC.
