How to Help

We are honored and excited to know that you want to help out Good for PoC.

There are a few ways you can help out.

Donate

Donations help us better the industry for people of color & create a more diverse workforce.

This isn’t work we can do by ourselves – we’re only a team of 3. If you appreciate the work we are doing, please consider supporting us by helping to cover labor and infrastructural costs of running this project.

You can donate to our cause once via PayPal or monthly via Patreon.

Volunteer

We’re always looking for people who are interested in helping out with Good for PoC. Either at events, adding content to the site, or with spreading the word.

If you’re interested in helping us out, shoot us an email with details about yourself and why you want to help out.
